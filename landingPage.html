Who are we?

Team HALO is an ambition-driven student team using drones to make an impact.

Consisting of students with experience in robotics competitions, this team was formed with the aim of winning one of India's biggest drone events — NIDAR 2025.

This team is competing in the Disaster Management Mission, focused on identifying trapped survivors and delivering aid in a simulated flood environment.
